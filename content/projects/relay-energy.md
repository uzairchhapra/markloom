Relay gives project teams one place to understand what is blocked, what needs attention, and what customers have been told.

## The challenge

Every installation crossed six organizations and dozens of handoffs. Teams were spending more time reconstructing project status than moving work forward.

## My role

I mapped the operational model with installers and account teams, designed the milestone system, and built the first production interface in close partnership with two back-end engineers.

## Outcome

- Gave account teams a shared source of truth across 1,800 active installations
- Reduced weekly status preparation by roughly eight hours per manager
- Created a component library now used across four internal products
