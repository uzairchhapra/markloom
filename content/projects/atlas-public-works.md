Atlas replaced a patchwork of spreadsheets, paper work orders, and disconnected resident reports with one shared operational view.

## The challenge

Crews needed a fast tool that worked outdoors and under pressure. Supervisors needed reliable status data. Residents needed clearer expectations without learning municipal workflows.

## My role

I led workflow research, interaction design, prototyping, and the front-end design system. The team shipped the first pilot in fourteen weeks, then expanded it across three departments.

## Outcome

- Reduced duplicate service requests by 31%
- Cut daily planning time from 45 minutes to under 12 minutes
- Met WCAG 2.2 AA across field and resident experiences
